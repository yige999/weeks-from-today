Pregnancy Calculator Guide: Using Weeks From Today for Pregnancy Tracking

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on using our Weeks From Today Calculator for pregnancy tracking. Whether you're trying to conceive, currently pregnant, or helping someone else track their pregnancy journey, our week calculator makes it easy to calculate important dates and milestones throughout pregnancy.

Understanding Pregnancy Weeks Calculation

Pregnancy is typically calculated in weeks, with a full-term pregnancy lasting approximately 40 weeks from the first day of the last menstrual period (LMP). Our weeks calculator helps you:

  • Calculate your due date based on conception or LMP
  • Track trimester milestones and important dates
  • Plan for prenatal appointments and tests
  • Count down to important pregnancy milestones

How to Calculate Pregnancy Due Date

Using our weeks calculator for pregnancy tracking is simple:

1

Determine Your Start Date

Use the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP) as your starting date. This is typically how healthcare providers calculate pregnancy due dates.

2

Calculate Key Pregnancy Weeks

Use our calculator to find dates for important milestones:

  • 12 weeks: End of first trimester
  • 20 weeks: Midway point, anatomy scan
  • 28 weeks: Third trimester begins
  • 40 weeks: Full-term due date
3

Track Your Progress

Use our calculator regularly to track how many weeks pregnant you are and count down to important milestones.

Excel Pregnancy Calculator Formula

If you prefer using Excel or Google Sheets for pregnancy tracking, you can use these formulas:

Due Date Calculation

=A1 + 280

Where A1 contains your LMP date (280 days = 40 weeks)

Current Pregnancy Week

=INT((TODAY()-A1)/7)

Calculates how many weeks pregnant you are

Weeks Until Due Date

=INT((B1-TODAY())/7)

Where B1 contains your due date
